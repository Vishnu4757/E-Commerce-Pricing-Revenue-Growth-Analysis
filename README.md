# 🛒 E-Commerce Pricing & Revenue Growth Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

- This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce pricing strategies, customer behavior, and revenue trends to uncover actionable business insights. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, discount impact, customer segmentation, and time-based trends.
- The goal is to help businesses optimize pricing strategies, improve revenue, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze revenue growth across different product categories
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on sales and revenue
- Understand customer behavior and demographics
- Identify seasonal and time-based sales trends
- Compare performance across regions and states
- Assess inventory pressure and competition impact

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Power BI / Tableau (Dashboard Visualization)
- Jupyter Notebook
- Data Visualization Techniques
- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

--- 

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains transactional e-commerce data including:

- Order details
- Product categories
- Pricing & discount information
- Customer demographics
- Time and location data

### 🔑 Key Insights
- Higher discounts do not always guarantee higher revenue
- Premium products generate strong revenue despite lower volume
- Certain states consistently outperform others in sales
- Seasonal events significantly boost revenue
- Inventory pressure directly affects sales performance
